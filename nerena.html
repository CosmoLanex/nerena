Our Story

A daughter's name,
a mother's hands.

Nérena began the way most lasting things do — quietly, at a kitchen table, with thread and a name. Our founder gave her daughter the name Nérena, and in the years that followed, found herself returning to the same word for the craft she was building: something gentle, something hers, something that would outlast a trend.

What started as handwoven gifts for family became a small workshop of artisans who share one belief — that an object made slowly, by hand, carries the warmth of the people who made it. Every Nérena piece is still cut, woven, and stitched the way the first one was: with care that cannot be automated.
